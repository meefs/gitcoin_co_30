## What is a Holon?

A holon represents "the whole that is simultaneously a part" — derived from Greek meaning "whole/parts." Holons describe the nested circles and communities individuals participate in simultaneously, from professional organizations to volunteer groups to digital networks.

![Holon concept diagram showing nested wholes and parts](/content-images/research/why-i-am-holon-maximalist/holon-concept-diagram.png)

## Holarchies

![Holarchies as nested systems](/content-images/research/why-i-am-holon-maximalist/holarchies-nested-systems.png)

Holarchies create connections between multiple holons:

- Systems within systems
- Stories within stories
- Communities within communities
- Networks within networks
- Contexts within contexts
- Identities within identities

## Application to Gitcoin Grants

### QF Rounds as Holons

![Quadratic Funding round structure as holons](/content-images/research/why-i-am-holon-maximalist/qf-round-structure.png)

Gitcoin's QF rounds function as holons within larger ecosystems:

- **Main Round:** Ethereum ecosystem (whole unto itself, part of open source ecosystem)
- **Cause Rounds:** Individual causes like Climate (whole in themselves, part of broader economy)
- **Side Rounds:** Protocol-specific rounds like Uniswap (whole entities, part of Web3 ecosystem)

### Multiplicative Matching Benefits

![Multiplicative matching benefits across multiple QF rounds](/content-images/research/why-i-am-holon-maximalist/multiplicative-matching-across-rounds.jpeg)

Grants benefit from participation across multiple holons. If a grant gets $250 matching from Holon A, $500 from B, $250 from C, $200 from D — it could get $1,200 match for a $1 contribution.

This stacking effect incentivizes expanding QF across numerous communities simultaneously.

## Strategic Vision: Holon Maximalism

The maximalist approach involves expanding the number of holons worldwide that implement QF matching. Objectives include:

- Creating a world where QF enhances every income stream
- Building civilizational-scale infrastructure for public goods
- Reaching "Quadratic Lands" — an economy augmented to better support public goods
- Achieving coordination at civilizational scale

## Key Insight

Individuals simultaneously belong to multiple overlapping communities, each capable of implementing QF. By maximizing QF adoption across these diverse holons, we can create compounding funding benefits for public goods across society.
