**TLDR** — A deep dive into designing, customizing, and deploying onchain capital allocation mechanisms. This Innovators Edition moves beyond introductory material to focus on creation and refinement for advanced practitioners.

## Overview

The Onchain Capital Allocation Handbook Volume 1 is a comprehensive guide for those designing the future of onchain coordination. It targets advanced users who already understand core concepts — protocol developers, DAO architects, and mechanism designers looking to build funding systems for protocol treasuries, grants programs, or new allocation infrastructure.

## What It Covers

- **Composability and modular design** — How to build allocation systems from reusable components
- **Mechanism tuning nuances** — Fine-tuning parameters for specific community contexts
- **Design patterns** — Matching pools, reputation layers, hybrid voting models, dynamic funding logic
- **Mechanism tradeoffs and design tensions** — Understanding the inherent tensions in allocation design
- **Implementation pathways** — Real-world examples and practical deployment guidance using Allo Protocol

## Who It's For

This edition is designed for advanced users who already understand the core concepts of onchain capital allocation. It's ideal for protocol developers building allocation infrastructure, DAO architects designing governance systems, and mechanism designers exploring new funding paradigms.
